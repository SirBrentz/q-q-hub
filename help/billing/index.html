Billing & Pricing

Learn how Quidget charges work, how to set your own prices, configure refunds, and maximize your profit margins.

How It Works Margins Refunds Return Fees Your Costs

How Pricing Works

Quidget uses a simple, transparent pay-as-you-go pricing model. You set the patient-facing price on your WooCommerce products, and Qualiphy charges you per consultation on our end. The difference is your profit.

The Payment Flow

  1. Patient purchases a consultation product on your WooCommerce store at your price
  2. You collect payment from the patient via WooCommerce
  3. When the consultation is approved (patient completes exam, provider signs off), Qualiphy charges your account the per-consultation cost
  4. Your margin = Patient price minus Qualiphy per-consultation cost

Pricing Structure

  • No signup fees — Start immediately with no onboarding costs
  • No monthly fees — Never pay a recurring platform fee
  • No contracts — Cancel anytime with no penalties
  • Pay per consultation — Only pay when a consultation actually happens
  • Default cost: $27.99 per consultation (varies by exam type)

Setting Up Pricing During Import

When you use the Import Consultation Wizard, the pricing step shows:

  • The default Qualiphy per-consultation cost (varies by type)
  • A suggested patient-facing price (cost + default 10% margin)
  • The ability to override with your own price

You're in full control — set any price you want for your patients.

Setting Price Margins

Your price margin is the profit you keep for each consultation. Quidget makes it easy to see and control your margins.

Default Price Margin

The default price margin is 10%, but you can configure it to any percentage from 0% to 500% in Qualiphy → Settings → Product.

How Margins Work During Import

When you import a consultation using the wizard:

  1. The system shows the Qualiphy per-consultation cost
  2. It auto-calculates a suggested price = Cost + margin percentage
  3. You can accept the suggested price or enter your own
  4. The margin is displayed so you always see exactly what you're earning

Example Margin Calculation

Suppose a consultation type costs $27.99 from Qualiphy:

  • With 10% margin: Patient price = $30.79 (you keep $2.80 per consultation)
  • With 25% margin: Patient price = $34.99 (you keep $7.00 per consultation)
  • With 50% margin: Patient price = $41.99 (you keep $14.00 per consultation)

Adjusting Margins Per Product

You can set different prices and margins for different consultation types:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Edit the product
  3. Set your desired Regular Price in the pricing section
  4. Update the product

The cost is always displayed alongside your price so you know your exact margin for each product.

Auto-Refund Logic

When a provider defers a consultation, Quidget can automatically issue a partial refund to protect your patient. Here's how it works.

When Auto-Refund Triggers

Auto Refund Rejected is enabled by default. When enabled, a refund is automatically issued whenever a provider defers (or rejects) a consultation.

  • The patient is refunded: Order total minus the configured Return Fee
  • The refund is issued via WooCommerce refund system (original payment method)
  • A detailed breakdown note is added to the refund for your records

Multiple Line Items

If a customer purchases multiple consultation products in one order and a deferral occurs:

  • Return fees are summed across all line items
  • For example: 2 products with $30 return fees each = $60 total retained
  • Refund = Total order amount minus $60

Example Auto-Refund Scenario

Order Details:

  • Customer purchases 1 consultation at $49.99
  • Payment processed and exam sent
  • Provider defers the consultation

Auto-Refund Calculation (with $30 return fee):

  • Order total: $49.99
  • Return fee retained: $30.00
  • Refund issued to patient: $19.99

Refund Note Details

When a refund is created, WooCommerce automatically adds a note like:

"Partial refund issued for deferred consultation. Retained $30.00 telehealth visit fee."

This keeps your team and customers informed about why they received a partial refund.

Disabling Auto-Refund

To disable auto-refunds (not recommended):

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Checkout Settings
  2. Toggle Auto Refund Rejected to OFF
  3. Save changes

Return Fee Configuration

Return fees are the amount you retain when a consultation is deferred. This compensates you for the service interaction while protecting the patient with a partial refund.

Default Return Fee

The default return fee is $30.00 per consultation, set globally in Qualiphy → Settings → Checkout Settings.

Setting Global Return Fee

To change the default return fee that applies to all new products:

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Checkout Settings
  2. Find Default Exam Price Retained
  3. Enter your desired fee amount (e.g., $25.00 or $50.00)
  4. Save changes

Overriding Per Product

You can set different return fees for different consultation types:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Edit the product
  3. In the Qualiphy section, set the Exam Price Retained (override the default)
  4. Update the product

This is useful if different exam types have different service costs to compensate for.

Return Fee Messaging

You can optionally display a disclaimer to patients about the return fee:

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Checkout Settings
  2. Toggle Show Exam Refund Message to ON
  3. The product page will display: "If your consultation is not approved, you will receive a refund minus the telehealth visit fee."

This sets customer expectations upfront about partial refunds.

When Return Fees Apply

Return fees are applied and retained when:

  • A provider defers a consultation (pending review)
  • A provider rejects a consultation
  • A subscription renewal is deferred

Return fees are not applied if a consultation is approved, as the full service was completed.

Understanding Your Costs

Quidget's cost structure is transparent and simple. You only pay Qualiphy when a consultation actually happens.

What You Pay

  • Per consultation charge: $27.99 (varies by exam type)
  • When: Only when a provider approves the exam and it's completed
  • Frequency: Varies based on your sales and renewals

What You Don't Pay

  • No plugin installation fee
  • No monthly subscription to use Quidget
  • No setup or onboarding fees
  • No contract or long-term commitments
  • No payment processing fees (WooCommerce charges those separately)
  • No charges for deferred or rejected consultations

Viewing Your Costs

During product setup (import wizard pricing step):

  1. The Qualiphy per-consultation cost is displayed
  2. Your customer-facing price is shown
  3. Your margin is calculated and displayed
  4. You can see exactly what you'll earn per consultation

Margin Example

If a consultation costs Qualiphy $27.99 and you set your patient-facing price at $49.99:

  • You collect: $49.99 from the patient
  • You pay Qualiphy: $27.99 per approved consultation
  • Your profit per consultation: $22.00
  • Your margin: 44% (very reasonable for telehealth)

Maximizing Your Margin

  • Keep per-consultation costs steady at Qualiphy's standard rate
  • Set competitive patient-facing prices (research your market)
  • Higher prices = higher margins, but may reduce conversion
  • Lower prices = more sales volume, but lower per-consultation profit
  • Offer bundles or packages to encourage higher order values
