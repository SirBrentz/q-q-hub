Billing & Pricing

Learn how Quidget pricing works, how to set competitive margins, configure refund logic, and understand your consultation costs.

How Pricing Works

Quidget uses a simple, transparent per-consultation pricing model with no hidden fees, contracts, or monthly minimums. You only pay when a consultation actually occurs.

The Pricing Model

Here's how the pricing structure works:

  • Base Cost: Qualiphy charges a per-consultation cost (default $27.99, may vary by exam type)
  • Patient-Facing Price: You set the price customers see on your WooCommerce store
  • Your Margin: The difference between what you charge and what Qualiphy charges is your profit
  • No Other Fees: No setup fees, no monthly subscription to Quidget, no transaction fees beyond the per-consultation cost

Example Pricing Breakdown

Let's say a consultation exam costs Qualiphy $27.99 to deliver. You want a healthy margin, so you decide to charge customers $49.99:

  • Customer pays: $49.99
  • Qualiphy cost: $27.99
  • Your margin: $22.00 per consultation (44%)

Per-Consultation Costs by Type

Different consultation types may have different costs. For example:

  • GFE (General Free Exam): Lower cost, typically $22.00
  • Rx (Prescription): Higher cost, typically $27.99-$35.00
  • Specialty Exams: May vary depending on provider and complexity

Check your specific exam types in Qualiphy → Consultations to see the exact base cost for each type.

No Upfront or Recurring Charges

Unlike other platforms, Quidget doesn't charge you for:

  • Monthly subscription or platform fees
  • Setup or activation fees
  • API usage or integration fees
  • Inactive merchant accounts

You're only charged when a consultation is successfully completed and the exam is approved or deferred. Rejected consultations are not charged.

Payment & Billing Cycle

Consultation costs are automatically deducted from your Qualiphy account balance. You can set up automatic recurring payments in your Qualiphy account to maintain a positive balance. Your billing history is always available at Qualiphy (clinic dashboard) → Settings → Billing → Invoice History.

Setting Price Margins

Your profit on each consultation is determined by the gap between the Qualiphy base cost and the price you set for customers. Quidget makes it easy to manage these margins.

Default Price Margin Setting

Quidget includes a configurable default price margin that auto-fills when you create or import products. By default, this is set to 10%, which means the suggested customer price is the Qualiphy cost plus 10%.

To adjust this setting:

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → General
  2. Find the Default Price Margin field
  3. Enter your desired margin percentage (e.g., 15, 25, 50)
  4. Click Save Changes

This margin percentage will be used when you import new consultations or create new products.

Pricing During Consultation Import

When you import a consultation type from Qualiphy into WooCommerce, the pricing step shows you:

  • Qualiphy Cost: The base cost for this consultation type (e.g., $27.99)
  • Your Margin %: The default margin percentage applied (e.g., 10%)
  • Suggested Price: The calculated customer price (e.g., $30.79)

Example Pricing Calculation

During import, you see:

  • Qualiphy Cost: $27.99
  • Margin: 10%
  • Calculation: $27.99 × 1.10 = $30.79 (suggested price)

Overriding the Suggested Price

The suggested price is just a starting point — you can set any price you want:

  • You can charge less than the suggested price (lower margin)
  • You can charge more for competitive positioning (higher margin)
  • Different pricing for different consultation types is fully supported

For example, you might charge $39.99 instead of the suggested $30.79 to build in more margin for marketing and operational costs.

Editing Prices After Import

After importing a consultation product, you can edit the price at any time:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Find your consultation product
  3. In the Product Data section, edit the Regular Price
  4. Click Update to save

Price changes take effect immediately for new orders. Existing orders are unaffected.

Viewing Your Margins

To see the margin on any product:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Edit the consultation product
  3. Look at the Qualiphy section, which shows:
    • Consultation Type (and its base cost)
    • Your Set Price
    • Profit per consultation

Competitive Pricing Tips

When deciding on pricing:

  • Research competitors: Check what other telehealth platforms charge for similar consultations
  • Consider market positioning: Premium branding may support higher prices; value positioning may require lower prices
  • Factor in overhead: Include marketing, support, payment processing, and operational costs in your margin calculation
  • Test and adjust: You can change prices anytime to optimize conversion and profitability
  • Promotional pricing: Use WooCommerce's sale pricing feature to run discounts without changing the base price

Refund Logic & Return Fees

Quidget automatically handles refunds for deferred or rejected consultations while protecting your business with configurable return fees.

Auto Refund on Rejection & Deferral

By default, when a consultation is rejected or deferred, Quidget issues an automatic partial refund to the customer. This setting is called Auto Refund Rejected and is enabled by default.

How Refunds Are Calculated

When a refund is issued, the calculation is:

Refund Amount = Order Total − Return Fee

This allows you to keep a portion of the payment to cover costs (provider review time, administrative overhead, etc.) while refunding most of the consultation fee.

Example Refund Scenario

  • Customer purchases a consultation for $49.99
  • Provider reviews and defers the consultation
  • Return fee: $30.00 (configured)
  • Refund to customer: $49.99 − $30.00 = $19.99
  • Amount retained by you: $30.00

What is a Return Fee?

A return fee is the amount Quidget retains from a consultation order when a refund is issued for a rejection or deferral. This covers:

  • Provider time spent reviewing the consultation
  • Pharmacy/system administration costs
  • Payment processing costs incurred
  • Customer service for handling the deferral or rejection

Configuring Return Fees

Return fees are configured on a per-product basis. Different consultation types can have different return fees.

To set a return fee:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Edit your consultation product
  3. In the Qualiphy section, set the Return Fee amount (default $30.00)
  4. Click Update to save

Return Fee Examples

  • Basic consultations: $20.00 return fee
  • Standard consultations: $30.00 return fee (default)
  • Premium/complex consultations: $50.00 return fee

Multiple Items in an Order

When a customer orders multiple consultation products, return fees are summed:

  • Customer orders 2 consultations at $49.99 each = $99.98 total
  • Each has a $30.00 return fee
  • Consultation 1 deferred: Refund $49.99 − $30.00 = $19.99
  • Consultation 2 deferred: Refund $49.99 − $30.00 = $19.99
  • Total refunded: $39.98 | Total retained: $60.00

Disabling Auto Refunds

If you prefer to handle refunds manually (not recommended), you can disable auto-refunds:

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Subscriptions (for subscriptions) or General (for all orders)
  2. Toggle Auto Refund Rejected to OFF
  3. Click Save Changes

When disabled, you'll need to manually refund deferred/rejected consultations in WooCommerce.

Manual Refund Overrides

Even with auto-refunds enabled, you can manually adjust or override any refund:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Orders
  2. Find the order with the deferred consultation
  3. In the Order Details section, click Refund
  4. Enter the custom refund amount and reason
  5. Click Refund $XX.XX to process

Refund Processing & Timing

Refunds are processed immediately to the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refund appears within 2-5 business days
  • PayPal: Refund appears within 1-2 business days
  • Bank Transfers: Refund may take longer depending on your bank

The customer receives an email notification when the refund is processed, and you can see refund history in the order details.

Understanding Your Costs

Clarity on what you pay helps you forecast profitability and plan your pricing strategy. Quidget keeps costs transparent.

When You're Charged

You are charged for a consultation in these scenarios:

  • Exam Submitted: Patient completes the exam questionnaire and submits it for review
  • Provider Review: When the provider begins reviewing (or has reviewed) the exam

You are NOT charged for:

  • Exam invites sent but not completed
  • Exams that are rejected by the provider
  • Exams that are deferred (deferral charges are reapplied only when the exam is re-submitted)

Viewing Your Consultation Costs

At every step, you can see the cost breakdown:

During Product Import

When importing a consultation, you see the cost in the pricing step. This is the per-consultation charge you'll incur when a patient purchases.

On the WooCommerce Product Page

Edit any consultation product to see:

  • Consultation Type: The Qualiphy exam type
  • Qualiphy Cost: What you'll pay per consultation
  • Your Price: What you're charging customers
  • Profit Per Sale: Your margin at a glance

In Your Qualiphy Clinic Dashboard

View detailed cost breakdowns anytime:

  1. Log into your Qualiphy clinic account at app.qualiphy.me
  2. Go to Settings → Billing → Invoice History
  3. View all past consultations and their costs
  4. Download invoices for accounting/tax purposes

Cost Management Tips

  • Monitor margins: Regularly review your pricing vs. costs to ensure healthy margins
  • Track conversion: Use WooCommerce analytics to see conversion rates by product price
  • Plan for deferred/rejected: Budget for the return fees you'll retain on deferred exams
  • Factor in payment processing: Your payment gateway (Stripe, PayPal) also charges a fee (~2.9% + $0.30), which comes from your margin
  • Review quarterly: Check your Qualiphy invoices quarterly to forecast annual costs and profitability

Payment Methods & Recurring Billing

Set up automatic recurring payments in your Qualiphy account to ensure consultation charges never exceed your balance:

  1. Log into app.qualiphy.me
  2. Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Methods
  3. Add your credit card or bank account
  4. Enable Automatic Recurring Payment to top up your balance automatically
