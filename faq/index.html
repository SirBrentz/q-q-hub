Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Quidget, setup, pricing, and compliance.

General

Quidget is a WordPress plugin that turns your WooCommerce store into a telehealth platform. It integrates with the Qualiphy provider network to offer automated exam invites, provider connections, prescriptions, and pharmacy fulfillment.

Yes. Quidget connects to the Qualiphy API. Sign up free at app.qualiphy.me/signup. No signup fees or contracts.

48 states + D.C. Alabama and Mississippi are currently excluded.

Yes. Qualiphy is HIPAA compliant and LegitScript certified. Patient data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

Pricing & Billing

The plugin itself is free. You pay per consultation — $27.99 per consultation (may vary by exam type). No signup fees, no monthly fees, no contracts.

Yes. You set any price on your WooCommerce products. The difference between your price and Qualiphy's per-consultation cost is your margin.

If a consultation is deferred, an automatic partial refund is issued (order total minus the return fee). Return fee defaults to $30 and is configurable per product.

Setup & Configuration

Most stores are live within 30 minutes. The setup wizard guides you through branding, service area, and consultation import.

Consultations & Exams

GFE (Good Faith Exam) is provider clearance for procedures like Botox or fillers. Rx is a full prescription consultation with pharmacy fulfillment.

Invites fire automatically when payment is confirmed. The patient receives an email and/or sees a popup on the thank-you page to connect with a provider.

You can resend the exam link from the order page: Order Actions → "Resend Consultation Link Email."

Providers are available 7 days a week, 365 days a year, 6AM–7PM PST.

Technical

WordPress 6.0+, WooCommerce 8.0+, PHP 7.4+. Compatible with most themes and page builders.

Quidget is designed to work alongside standard WooCommerce plugins. Occasionally caching or security plugins need minor configuration (see Troubleshooting).
