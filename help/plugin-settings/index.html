Plugin Settings

A complete reference for all Quidget plugin settings. Configure your checkout experience, branding, service area, and clinical controls.

General Settings

Access general settings from Qualiphy → Settings → General in your WordPress admin. These core settings control how exams are delivered and displayed to your patients.

Auto Send Exams

Default: Enabled

When enabled, the plugin automatically sends exam invites to patients immediately after they complete checkout and payment is confirmed. If disabled, you'll need to manually send exam invites from the order detail page.

Use case: Disable this if you want to manually review orders before sending exams, or if you need time to prepare before patients begin their consultation.

Auto Launch Exams

Default: Enabled

When enabled, the exam modal automatically opens on the thank-you page after checkout, allowing patients to start their exam immediately. If disabled, patients must click a link to open the exam modal.

Use case: Disable if you prefer a lighter, simpler thank-you page, or if you want patients to access exams from a separate link.

Exam Launch Mode

Options: Popup or Redirect

  • Popup (Modal): Opens the exam in a modal overlay with the meeting link and QR code. Patients can close the modal to return to the thank-you page.
  • Redirect: Navigates to a dedicated exam page with a countdown timer until the meeting starts. Used for more immersive exam experiences.

Recommended: Popup is best for most sites — it keeps patients on your page while allowing them to see the exam details.

Default Price Margin

Default: 10%

This percentage is automatically applied to Qualiphy consultation costs when pricing products. If a Qualiphy consultation costs $50, and your margin is 10%, the patient-facing price will be $55 (before tax).

Note: You can override the margin on a per-product basis when importing or editing consultations.

Checkout Settings

Access checkout settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Checkout. These settings control what information is collected from patients during the checkout process.

Additional Fields

Quidget automatically adds the following fields to checkout for all Qualiphy products:

  • Gender: Dropdown (Male, Female, Non-binary, Prefer not to say)
  • Date of Birth: Date picker
  • Phone Number: Phone input with formatting

These fields are required for the provider to conduct the consultation. They appear before the standard WooCommerce address fields.

Age Restriction

Age validation is automatically enforced based on the consultation type:

  • Rx consultations: Patient must be 18 or older
  • GFE consultations: Patient must be 13 or older

If a customer's date of birth shows they're too young, checkout is blocked and they'll see an error message: "This product is not available for your age group."

Account Creation

Guest checkout is disabled for all Quidget products to ensure proper order tracking and exam delivery. Customers must either log in to an existing account or create a new account before purchasing.

Auto Redirect to Checkout

Default: Enabled

When a customer adds a Quidget product to their cart, they're automatically redirected to checkout instead of seeing the cart page. This streamlines the purchase experience for consultations.

Disable if: You want customers to review their cart before checkout, or if you offer bundle products with multiple items.

Branding & White-Label

Customize the appearance of the exam modal and patient-facing pages to match your clinic's branding. Access branding settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Branding.

Clinic Logo

Upload your clinic logo to appear on the exam modal, patient emails, and checkout pages.

  • Recommended format: PNG with transparent background
  • Dimensions: 200x200px or larger (aspect ratio 1:1 or 2:1)
  • Dark variant: Upload a dark version of your logo for use on light backgrounds
  • Light variant: Upload a light version for use on dark backgrounds

Accent Colors

Choose two accent colors to customize the visual appearance of Quidget throughout the patient experience:

  • Primary Color: Default #CFC9FF (light purple). Used for buttons, links, accents, and the exam modal header.
  • Secondary Color: Default #6e54a7 (dark purple). Used for text, headings, and interactive elements.

Tip: Choose colors that complement your clinic's branding and have good contrast with white and dark backgrounds. Use a color picker tool like Coolers.co to find colors that work well together.

Applied Locations

Your branding is applied to:

  • Exam modal header and buttons
  • Exam thank-you page
  • Patient notification emails
  • Checkout page elements (section headers, accent lines)

Service Area Configuration

Define the states and territories where you're licensed to provide consultations. Access service area settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Service Area.

Supported Locations

Quidget supports consultations in 48 US states plus Washington, D.C. The following states are not currently available:

  • Alabama
  • Mississippi

Selecting Your Service Area

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Service Area
  2. Check the states where you're licensed to practice
  3. Click Save Service Area

Automatic Shipping Zone

When you save your service area, Quidget automatically creates a WooCommerce shipping zone containing your selected states. This zone is used for all Quidget products and ensures accurate location-based restrictions.

Location-Based Restrictions

Customers outside your service area will see an error message on the product page: "This product is not available in your area." They cannot add the product to their cart or proceed to checkout.

Note: Location is determined by the customer's shipping address at checkout. You can allow patients from outside your service area to proceed by temporarily expanding your service area.

Managing Your Service Area

If you expand into new states or need to restrict your service area, you can update it at any time from the settings page. The WooCommerce shipping zone is updated automatically.

Language & Custom Text

Customize all patient-facing text in the Quidget plugin to match your clinic's voice and messaging. Access language settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Language.

Customizable Text Sections

Rx vs. Non-Rx Messages

Customize the messaging shown to patients based on consultation type:

  • Rx intro text: Explanation of prescription consultations
  • GFE intro text: Explanation of aesthetic procedure consultations
  • Rx completion message: Text shown after Rx is approved
  • GFE completion message: Text shown after GFE is approved

Button Text

Customize button labels throughout the patient journey:

  • "Start Exam" → "Begin Consultation"
  • "Schedule Meeting" → "Talk with Provider"
  • "View Status" → "Check Progress"

Cart Messages

Customize messages shown on the cart and checkout pages:

  • Introductory text above Quidget products
  • Cart item description for consultation products
  • Checkout section header (e.g., "Patient Information")

Exam Modal Text

Customize text inside the exam launch modal:

  • Modal title and description
  • Meeting countdown text
  • QR code label and instructions

Questionnaire Questions

Customize the questions shown during the patient exam:

  • Medical history questions
  • Symptom and concern questions
  • Dose history questions (for returning patients)

Dose Labels

For consultation products that include dose selection, customize the dose option labels:

  • Label for starting dose (e.g., "Start with 0.25mg")
  • Label for maintenance dose (e.g., "Continue current dose")
  • Label for increased dose (e.g., "Increase to 0.5mg")

Disclaimer Text

Customize legal and medical disclaimers shown to patients:

  • General medical disclaimer (shown on product pages)
  • Exam disclaimer (shown before exam starts)
  • Prescription disclaimer (shown for Rx products)

Saving Changes

Click Save Language Settings to apply all text customizations. Changes take effect immediately on the patient-facing site — no cache clearing needed.

Clinical Access Controls

Restrict access to patient consultation data in WooCommerce to designated staff members. Access clinical controls from Qualiphy → Settings → Clinical Access.

How Clinical Access Works

By default, only users with the "Administrator" role can view patient consultation data in WooCommerce orders. Other users see a locked icon with the message "Patient Order Access Required."

Granting Access

To give a staff member access to patient consultation data:

  1. Go to Qualiphy → Settings → Clinical Access
  2. Check the names of users who should have access
  3. Click Save Access Controls

Note: The user who initially set up Quidget (connected the API key) automatically receives access and cannot be removed.

User Roles

You can grant access to users with any role:

  • Administrator: Full site access + consultation data
  • Shop Manager: Order and product management access
  • Editor: Content editing access
  • Custom roles: Any custom role you've created

Audit and Compliance

Quidget logs all access to patient data for compliance purposes. Access logs are available from Qualiphy → Settings → Access Logs.

Email Notifications

Configure which emails your team receives for consultation events. Access email settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Notifications.

Admin Notifications

Your team receives notifications for critical consultation events:

  • Exam Invite Failed: Notifies admins if an exam invite email fails to send to the patient
  • Exam Submitted: Notifies admins when a patient completes their exam questionnaire
  • Exam Approved: Notifies admins when a provider approves a consultation
  • Prescription Created: Notifies admins when a prescription is issued (Rx only)
  • Shipment Tracking: Notifies admins when pharmacy tracking updates

Admin Email Address

Admin notifications are sent to the email address configured in Settings → General → Site Email Address in WordPress. Change this to route notifications to a different address (e.g., a support inbox).

Patient Notifications

Patient emails are handled automatically by Quidget and cannot be disabled. Patients receive:

  • Exam Invite: Link to start their exam (sent immediately after purchase)
  • Exam Reminder: Reminder 1 hour before their scheduled meeting
  • Approval Notification: Confirmation that their consultation was approved
  • Shipment Tracking: Tracking number and link when medication ships (Rx only)

Email Customization

Patient email templates are customized from Qualiphy → Settings → Language (see the section on Language & Custom Text). You can modify the email subject lines, headers, and body text to match your clinic's branding.

Auto-Refund Settings

Configure automatic refund handling when a provider rejects a patient's consultation. Access auto-refund settings from Qualiphy → Settings → Refunds.

How Auto-Refund Works

If a provider rejects a patient's consultation (typically due to disqualifying health conditions or other clinical reasons), Quidget can automatically refund the patient minus a return fee.

Auto Refund Rejected Consultations

Default: Enabled

When enabled, rejected consultations automatically trigger a refund to the customer's original payment method. The refund is processed within 5-10 business days depending on the payment processor.

Return Fee

Default: $30.00

When a consultation is rejected, the customer receives a refund equal to the order total minus the return fee. For example:

  • Order total: $99.00
  • Return fee: $30.00
  • Refund amount: $69.00

Per-Product Override

You can set a different return fee for specific consultation products. This is useful if some consultations have higher costs and you want a different return fee percentage:

  1. Go to WooCommerce → Products
  2. Edit the consultation product
  3. Find the Quidget Settings section
  4. Enter a custom Return Fee (or leave blank to use the default)
  5. Save the product

Multiple Line Items

If an order contains multiple Quidget products, the return fee is summed across all line items. For example:

  • Item 1 (GFE): $50.00 → Refund $20.00 (return fee $30)
  • Item 2 (Rx): $100.00 → Refund $70.00 (return fee $30)
  • Total refund: $90.00 (total return fees: $60.00)

Disabling Auto-Refund

If you prefer to manually review rejections before issuing refunds, disable auto-refund from Qualiphy → Settings → Refunds. You can then issue refunds manually from the WooCommerce order detail page.

Refund Status

Track all refunds from WooCommerce → Orders. Each order shows refund status in the Qualiphy section, including:

  • Refund amount
  • Refund date
  • Refund reason (e.g., "Patient rejected")
  • Refund transaction ID
